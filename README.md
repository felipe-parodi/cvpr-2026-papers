Tracking interesting papers from CVPR 2026.

Template for multi-camera primate rigs. |
| WildCap: Facial Albedo Capture in the Wild via Hybrid Inverse Rendering | Han, Ming, Li, Shen, Zhang, Xu, Xu | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.11237) · [page](https://yxuhan.github.io/WildCap/) · [code](https://github.com/yxuhan/WildCap) | In-the-wild inverse rendering of facial albedo from phone video. The "wild" angle matters for naturalistic settings. |

| Occluded Facial Expression Recognition and Recovery | — | _link TBD_ | Reconstructs occluded expressions; for partially-visible faces in naturalistic video. No public link found yet. |
| TDMM-LM: Bridging Facial Understanding and Animation via Language Models | Song et al. | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16936) · [code/data](https://github.com/Songluchuan/TDMM-LM_data) · [page](https://songluchuan.github.io/TDMM-LM/) | Casts 3D facial params as a language problem (Motion2Language + Language2Motion). LM-mediated analysis↔generation bridge. |
| Face Time Traveller: Travel Through Ages Without Losing Identity | Kar et al. (Sony Research India) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22819) | Diffusion-based identity-preserving age transformation. |
| ExposeAnyone: Personalized Audio-to-Expression Diffusion Models Are Robust Zero-Shot Face Forgery Detectors | Shiohara, Yamasaki, Golyanik | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02359) · [page](https://mapooon.github.io/ExposeAnyonePage/) | Self-supervised audio→expression diffusion for deepfake detection. ⚠️ Diffusion-based, **not** VLM-based as originally noted. |
| GazeOnce360: Fisheye-Based 360° Multi-Person Gaze Estimation with Global-Local Feature Fusion | Cai, Sun, Lu | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17161) · [code](https://github.com/caizhuojiang/GazeOnce360) | Upward fisheye, multi-person 360° 3D gaze; synthetic MPSGaze360 dataset. |

| Omni-MMSI: Toward Identity-attributed Social Interaction Understanding | Li et al. | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00267) · [page](https://sampson-lee.github.io/omni-mmsi-project-page/) | Identity-attributed social interaction from raw audio/vision/speech, in humans. |
| PercHead: Perceptual Head Model for Single-Image 3D Head Reconstruction & Editing | Oroz, Nießner, Kirschstein (TUM) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02777) · [code (soon)](https://github.com/antoniooroz/PercHead) · [page](https://antoniooroz.github.io/PercHead/) | Single-image 3D head reconstruction + semantic editing via Gaussian splatting, DINOv2/SAM2 perceptual supervision. Face/head geometry from limited views. |
| PhysHead: Simulation-Ready Gaussian Head Avatars | Kabadayi et al. | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.06467) · [code](https://github.com/bernakabadayi/physhead) | Sim-ready Gaussian head avatars with strand-based hair physics. Physically plausible renderings, not just pretty avatars. |
| FHAvatar: Fast and High-Fidelity Reconstruction of Face-and-Hair Composable 3D Head Avatar from Few Casual Captures | Sun, Cai et al. (SJTU/Alibaba) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23345) | Few-view face+hair composable 3D head avatars from casual phone shots, in minutes. Likely human-specific. |
| POLAR: A Portrait OLAT Dataset and Generative Framework for Illumination-Aware Face Modeling | Chen et al. (SJTU/PICO) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.13192) · [page](https://rex0191.github.io/POLAR/) | Portrait OLAT dataset (200+ subjects, 156 lights) + flow-based relighting. Disentangles identity/shape/illumination; controlled face stimuli & augmentations. |
| FACE: A Face-based Autoregressive Representation for High-Fidelity and Efficient Mesh Generation | Wang, Guo, Liu et al. | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.01515) | One-face-one-token autoregressive mesh generation (9× shorter sequences). Compact face geometry priors. |
| FlashLips: 100-FPS Mask-Free Latent Lip-Sync using Reconstruction Instead of Diffusion or GANs | Zinonos, Stypułkowski, Bigata et al. | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20033) | 100-FPS mask-free latent lip-sync via reconstruction (no diffusion/GAN). Audiovisual face dynamics; more speech/animation than social behavior. |
| EmoTaG: Emotion-Aware Talking Head Synthesis on Gaussian Splatting with Few-Shot Personalization | Xu, Cheng, Wang, Bi, Liu (MSU/Qualcomm/UNC) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21332) · [code](https://github.com/jamesdemon923/EmoTaG) | Emotion-aware 3D talking head on Gaussian splatting; 5s few-shot personalization in FLAME space. Disentangles identity/expression/dynamics. |
| PC-Talk: Precise Facial Animation Control for Audio-Driven Talking Face Generation | Wang, Zhu, Shen, Xu, Lei (CASIA) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.14295) · [page](https://bq-wang0511.github.io/PC-Talk/) | Precise audio-driven facial animation via implicit keypoints; word-level lip + emotion control. On arXiv since Mar 2025. |
| PerformRecast: Expression and Head Pose Disentanglement for Portrait Video Editing | Liang, Xiong et al. (HUJING) | [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19731) | Disentangles expression from head pose for portrait video editing (3DMM-based). Separable pose vs. expression latent factors. |
